PAPUTOK NI TASYO

Fireworks hold cultural, symbolic, and entertainment significance, playing a vital role in various celebrations and festivals worldwide. They symbolize joy, mark historical events, and contribute to community bonding, bringing people together for shared experiences. Beyond their aesthetic appeal and entertainment value, fireworks often attract tourism, stimulating local economies. While widely celebrated, the environmental and safety concerns associated with fireworks, such as pollution and fire hazards, have led to ongoing discussions about balancing their enjoyment with responsible use.
